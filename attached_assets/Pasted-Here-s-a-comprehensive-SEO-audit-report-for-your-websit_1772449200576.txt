📌 1. SEO‑Optimized Homepage Template (What Should Be There)

A strong homepage for SEO should include the following in its <head> section:

🔹 Required Elements (On‑Page SEO)
Element	Purpose
<title>	Main topic of the page; vital for rankings.
<meta name="description">	Short summary shown in search results (max ~155‑160 chars).
<meta name="robots" content="index,follow">	Tells bots to index and follow links.
<h1>	Main heading with primary keyword.
<h2>, <h3>	Subheadings for structure/content sections.
<link rel="canonical">	Prevents duplicate content issues.
<lang> attribute	Defines language for search engines.

Example SEO‑ready head section

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
  <title>Bio Green Wax Ltd – Edible Oils & Industrial Wax Supplier</title>
  <meta name="description" content="Supplier of RSPO MB/SG & EUDR-certified palm oils, olein, stearin and custom plant-based & paraffin wax blends for industries.">
  <meta name="robots" content="index,follow">
  <link rel="canonical" href="https://biogreenwax.com/">
</head>
<body>
<!-- page content -->
