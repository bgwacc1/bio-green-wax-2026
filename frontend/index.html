Bio Green Wax Ltd — Premium Edible Oils, Fats & Industrial Waxes

Bio Green Wax Ltd is a leading UK-based B2B supplier of premium edible oils, plant-based waxes, and industrial petrochemical waxes. We offer RSPO SG (Segregated) and MB (Mass Balance) certified palm derivatives, EUDR-compliant products, and custom wax blends for a wide range of industries.

Our Products

We supply a comprehensive range of oils and waxes including palm oil, palm olein, palm stearin, sunflower oil, rapeseed oil, coconut oil, soybean oil, paraffin wax, microcrystalline wax, slack wax, and beeswax. All products are available in bulk and custom specifications.

Industries We Serve

Our products serve food manufacturing, candle making, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, rubber and plastics, textiles, packaging, adhesives, polishes and coatings, and paper and printing industries worldwide.

Certifications & Sustainability

Bio Green Wax Ltd holds RSPO SG and MB certifications for sustainable palm oil supply, and all our palm derivatives are EUDR (EU Deforestation Regulation) compliant. We are committed to responsible sourcing and environmental sustainability.
